About TigerGainz

Why Was This Created?

Inspired by their own virtual COVID-19 workout sessions, Eugene Liu ’24, Vinay Konuru ’24, and Ben Cai ’24, wanted to share the joy of exercise with the rest of the Princeton community.

Management

TigerGainz is a workout buddy and group matchmaker created and currently managed by Vinay Konuru ’24, Eugene Liu ’24, Elizabeth Medina ’24, and Ben Cai ’24. The source code is available on GitHub.

FAQs

1. Who has access to my TigerGainz profile and sign-up responses? What is my data used for?

All your TigerGainz sign-up responses and profile are securely stored and only distributed to you and your prospective workout partners. The data is solely used to create workout buddy matches and is not shared with any other organizations.
