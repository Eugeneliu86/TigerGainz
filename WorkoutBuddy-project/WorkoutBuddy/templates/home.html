What is TigerGainz?

TigerGainz is a workout buddy and group matchmaker for Princetonians that:

1. Analyzes your workout preferences

2. Finds Princetonians with the most FITTING responses to yours

3. Gives you their contact information so you can reach out to them when you match

With an instantaneous and more accurate algorithm , TigerGainz removes the usual stress, uncertainty, and awkwardness that comes with finding workout buddies and groups.

As an added bonus, TigerGainz's directory permits you to see the profiles of all workout-buddy-or-group-interested Princetonians (not just those you matched with).

By finding the best workout buddies and groups, TigerGainz helps Princetonians stay active through better exercise experiences that are:

- more consistent because buddies and groups keep each other accountable

- of higher quality since studies show that working out with others can increase the duration and intensity by up to 200% (Kansas State University)

- more fun, whether you are on- or off-campus

How Does it Work?

Upon filling out the secure sign up form (see "Get Starated" button above), TigerGainz will use an algorithm that aligns preferences--including workout days, exercise type, time zone, and frequency--to find the most fitting workout partners (or groups!) from our database of participating Princetonians.

Once matches have been made, you’ll get to see the profiles, preferences, and, most importantly, contact information of your best potential workout buddies or groups.

Then, it’s up to you to reach out to your new workout buddy or group!

If you are satisfied with your matches and are no longer interested in finding Princeton workout buddies or groups, we will remove you from our database.

Conversely, if you are unsatisfied with or have no matches, TigerGainz will place you in its database and waiting list. Once a potential match is made, TigerGainz will notify you via email and give you the profiles, preferences, and contact information of your prospective workout buddies and groups. You can then decide whom to reach out to and whether or not you would like to remain in the TigerGainz database.

If you wish to be removed from the database, please click the "Take me off the database please!" button on the "database" page.
