What is TigerGainz?

TigerGainz is a workout buddy and group matchmaker for Princetonians that:

1. Analyzes your workout preferences

2. Finds Princetonians with the most similar responses to yours

3. Gives you their contact information so you can reach out to them when you match

With an instantaneous and more accurate algorithm, TigerGainz removes the usual stress, uncertainty, and awkwardness that comes with finding workout buddies and groups.

By finding the best workout buddies and groups, TigerGainz helps Princetonians stay active through better exercise experiences that are not only more consistent (because buddies and groups keep each other accountable) but more fun, whether you are on- or off-campus.
