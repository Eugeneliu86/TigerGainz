About TigerGainz

Why Was TigerGainz Created?

Amidst the COVID-19 pandemic and virtual classes, Vinay Konuru ’24 and I (Ben Cai ’24) had to find new ways to stay active. Unfortunately, each of our peers had different schedules, goals, exercise preferences, and so on; quarantine only exacerbated these conflicts. As a result, Vinay and I had to exercise on our own, and we struggled to keep ourselves motivated. Fortunately, Vinay and I met each other and other workout-eager classmates in the Class of 2024 Princeton group chat. After much planning, we were able to find suitable workout buddies and schedules that allowed us to meet our exercise goals and get to bond with our (new) peers.

But what if this process of finding workout buddies or groups with the most fitting workout partners was not only faster but more accessible?

That’s why Vinay and I, with the help of Eugene Liu ’24 and Elizabeth Medina ’24, co-founded TigerGainz to share the joy of exercise with the rest of the Princeton community.

Knowing that working out with others helps increase accountability and performance—studies have found that having a buddy can increase your workout time and intensity by as much as 200 percent—we hope TigerGainz will not only help Princetonians find the most fitting workout partners/groups, but it will help create a closer community on- and off-campus.

Ultimately, TigerGainz is a way to empower and connect Princetonians, and we wish all of you the best of luck.

Happy Exercising!

With much love,

The TigerGainz Gang: Ben, Vinay, Eugene, & Elizabeth

FAQs

Below are answers to FAQs. If you still have questions or concerns, please email one of the co-founders or complete our feedback form.

1. Who has access to my TigerGainz responses/workout preferences? What is my data used for?

All your TigerGainz responses/workout preferences and profile are securely stored and only available to Princeton students that have signed up for TigerGainz. Your prospective matches will see your profile and any Princeton student that has signed up for TigerGainz can see your workout profile in our directory even if they aren’t a match. The data is solely used to create workout buddy matches and is not shared with any other organizations.

2. My current Princeton workout buddy/group and I decided that we are not the best fit for each other. Can we request new Princeton workout buddies/groups? If so, how?

Yes, you can request new Princeton workout buddies/groups. Simply go onto the home page and click the sign up button to complete the form again (note: this will replace your old reponses). TigerGainz will find new matches for you again, and then you can choose who to reach out to.

3. Can I edit my workout preferences? If so, how?

Yes, you can edit your workout preferences. Simply go onto the home page and click the sign up button to complete the form again. Your new responses will replace your old ones. However, this also means you may get different matches.

If you would like to keep the same matches but want to indicate different workout preferences, just reach out to those matches and inform them of your new workout preferences. Hopefully, you can still work something out!

4. Why am I getting no matches? How long will I have to wait for a match?

In the unlikely scenario that TigerGainz finds no suitable matches - most likely due to uncommon workout preferences (e.g. specific days or workout lengths that not many other Princetonians are looking for) - you should consider adjusting your workout preferences. Being more flexible will likely result in more matches; plus, it’s a great way to meet different people.

To do this, please go onto the home page and click the sign up button to complete the form again (note: this will replace your old responses). Hopefully, if there are still Princetonians looking for a workout buddy or group, you should instantly get matches.

Regarding the wait time for matches: typically, at the beginning of each semester when everyone is signing up, it is less than a day if not instantaneous. As the semester progresses though, you may have to wait several days since many people will have presumably found matches and taken themself off our database.

5. I haven’t heard back from TigerGainz in a while and I’m still looking for a match. What should I do?

If you are still looking for a match and TigerGainz has not reached out to you, that probably means that there are either no closely suitable Princetonians or no Princetonians looking for matches at all. The latter reason is highly unlikely though.

In any case, you should consider adjusting your workout preferences. Being more flexible will likely result in more matches; plus, it’s a great way to meet different people.

To do this, please go onto the home page and click the sign up button to complete the form again (note: this will replace your old responses). Hopefully, if there are still Princetonians looking for a workout buddy or group, you should instantly get matches.

6. How can I maximize my chances of getting a match?

To maximize your chances of getting a match, you should select as many workout preferences you are comfortable with and choose the “average” response when applicable. For example, in terms of availability, if you are free more days, you will have more options. On the other hand, for preferences like workout length, you should select a more common option like “1 hour” as opposed to “2 hours.” In any case, being flexible will grant you the most matches.

7. Am I able to view other people’s profiles even if I haven’t matched with them?

Yes, anyone who has logged in with a Princeton email can view your profile via the TigerGainz directory even if they aren’t a match or haven’t signed up for TigerGainz. As such, please be mindful of what you write.
