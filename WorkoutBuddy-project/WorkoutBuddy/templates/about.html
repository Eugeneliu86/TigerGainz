About TigerGainz

Why Was TigerGainz Created?

Amidst the COVID-19 pandemic and virtual classes, Ben Cai ’24 and Vinay Konuru ’24 had to find new ways to stay active. In particular, they still wanted to train with their teammates and friends. Unfortunately, each of their peers had different schedules, goals, exercise preferences, and so on; quarantine only exacerbated these conflicts. As a result, Ben and Vinay had to exercise on their own, and they struggled to keep themselves motivated. Fortunately, Ben and Vinay met each other and other workout-eager classmates in the Class of 2024 Princeton group chat.

But what if you could find other Princetonians who had the same workout preferences as you?

That’s why Ben and Vinay, with the help of Eugene Liu ’24 and Elizabeth Medina ’24, created TigerGainz to share the joy of exercise with the rest of the Princeton community. They hope the website will not only help Princetonians find the most fitting workout partners/groups who will keep them accountable.

Management

TigerGainz is a workout buddy and group matchmaker created and currently managed by Vinay Konuru ’24, Eugene Liu ’24, Elizabeth Medina ’24, and Ben Cai ’24. The source code is available on GitHub.

FAQs

1. Who has access to my TigerGainz profile and sign-up responses? What is my data used for?

All your TigerGainz sign-up responses and profile are securely stored and only distributed to you and your prospective workout partners. The data is solely used to create workout buddy matches and is not shared with any other organizations.
