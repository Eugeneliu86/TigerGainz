About TigerGainz

Why Was TigerGainz Created?

Amidst the COVID-19 pandemic and virtual classes, Vinay Konuru ’24 and I (Ben Cai ’24) had to find new ways to stay active. Unfortunately, each of our peers had different schedules, goals, exercise preferences, and so on; quarantine only exacerbated these conflicts. As a result, Vinay and I had to exercise on our own, and we struggled to keep ourselves motivated. Fortunately, Vinay and I met each other and other workout-eager classmates in the Class of 2024 Princeton group chat. After much planning, we were able to find suitable workout buddies and schedules that allowed us to meet our exercise goals and get to bond with our (new) peers.

But what if this process of finding workout buddies or groups with the most fitting workout partners was not only faster but more accessible?

That’s why Vinay and I, with the help of Eugene Liu ’24 and Elizabeth Medina ’24, co-founded TigerGainz to share the joy of exercise with the rest of the Princeton community.

Knowing that working out with others helps increase accountability and performance—studies have found that having a buddy can increase your workout time and intensity by as much as 200 percent—we hope TigerGainz will not only help Princetonians find the most fitting workout partners/groups, but it will help create a closer community on- and off-campus.

Ultimately, TigerGainz is a way to empower and connect Princetonians, and we wish all of you the best of luck.

Happy Exercising!

With much love,

The TigerGainz Gang: Ben, Vinay, Eugene, & Elizabeth

FAQs

Below are answers to FAQs. If you still have questions or concerns, please email one of the co-founders or complete our feedback form.

1. Who has access to my TigerGainz responses/workout preferences? What is my data used for?

All your TigerGainz responses/workout preferences and profile are securely stored and only available to Princeton students that have logged in with their Princeton gmail. Your prospective matches will see your profile and any Princeton student can see your workout profile in our directory even if they aren’t a match. The data is solely used to create workout buddy matches and is not shared with any other organizations.

2. My current Princeton workout buddy/group and I decided that we are not the best fit for each other/Someone chose me as their workout buddy but they are not one of my matches and/or I am not sure if I want to be their workout buddy. Can we request new Princeton workout buddies/groups? If so, how?

Although it can be awkward, sometimes the TigerGainz matches don’t work out but that’s okay! If you would still like to search for workout buddies (or groups), politely tell the person who chose you as their partner you situation (they should be understanding!).

Then, both of you should request new Princeton workout buddies/groups. Simply go onto the home page and click the sign up button to complete the form again (note: this will replace your old reponses). TigerGainz will find new matches for you again, and then you can choose who to reach out to.

Currently, the algorithm takes both people off the database when only one person “Accepts” a match; this will be resolved in the future. Additionally an option to decline a match offer from another Princetonian will be implemented.

3. Can I edit my workout preferences? If so, how?

Yes, you can edit your workout preferences. To do this, please go to the Database section and click the ““Take me off the database please” button. Then, click the sign up button to complete the form again (note: this will replace your old responses). However, this also means you may get different matches.

If you would like to keep the same matches but want to indicate different workout preferences, please PERSONALLY reach out to those matches (via your own email; not TigerGainz) and inform them of your new workout preference; you can find their contact info under their profile. Please do NOT “Accept” a match via TigerGainz as this will remove the other person from the database even though they have not been paired with someone who has the same workout preferences. Hopefully, you can still work something out!

4. Why am I getting no matches on my initial search? What should I do? How long will I have to wait for a match?

In the unlikely scenario that TigerGainz finds no suitable matches upon form submission - most likely due to uncommon workout preferences (e.g. specific days or workout lengths that not many other Princetonians are looking for) - you should consider adjusting your workout preferences and resubmitting the form. Being more flexible will likely result in more matches; plus, it’s a great way to meet different people.

To resubmit your form, please go to the Database section and click the “Take me off the database please” button. Then, click the sign up button to complete the form again (note: this will replace your old responses). Hopefully, if there are still Princetonians looking for a workout buddy or group, you should instantly get matches.

On the other hand, if you have already submitted the form and are still not getting matches, that means someone hasn’t chosen you to be their workout buddy or group yet.

This is especially common as the semester progresses where you might have to wait several days since many people will have presumably found matches and taken themself off our database. At the beginning of each semester when everyone is signing up, the match time is less than a day if not instantaneous.

In any case, if you don’t want to wait for someone to choose you, please resubmit your form (see above) or check out the TigerGainz directory.

5. I haven’t heard back from TigerGainz in a while and I’m still looking for a match. What should I do?

If you are still looking for a match and TigerGainz has not reached out to you, that probably means that someone hasn’t chosen you as their workout partner.

As such, you should consider resubmitting the workout form. To do this, please go to the Database section and click the ““Take me off the database please” button. Then, click the sign up button to complete the form again (note: this will replace your old responses).

Hopefully, if there are still Princetonians looking for a workout buddy or group, you should instantly get matches. Alternatively, you can check out the TigerGainz directory.

6. How can I maximize my chances of getting a match?

To maximize your chances of getting a match, you should select as many workout preferences you are comfortable with and choose the “average” response when applicable. For example, in terms of availability, if you are free more days, you will have more options. On the other hand, for preferences like workout length, you should select a more common option like “1 hour” as opposed to “2 hours.” In any case, being flexible will grant you the most matches.

7. Am I able to view other people’s profiles even if I haven’t matched with them?

Yes, anyone who has logged in with a Princeton email can view your profile via the TigerGainz directory even if they aren’t a match or haven’t signed up for TigerGainz. As such, please be mindful of what you write.
