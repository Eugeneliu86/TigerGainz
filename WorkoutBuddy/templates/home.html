What is TigerGainz?

TigerGainz is a workout buddy and group*** matchmaker for Princetonians that:

1. Analyzes your workout preferences and stores them in our TigerGainz directory

2. Finds Princetonians with the most FITTING workout preferences to yours

3. Gives you their profiles so you can decide who to reach out to

4. Pairs you with your new workout buddy (or group) IF you “Accept” a match and sends confirmation emails to you and your buddy. *This also means that you might receive a “random” email from a new workout buddy who has decided to choose you.

5. Gets you started on your exercising adventure!

With an instantaneous and accurate algorithm , TigerGainz removes the usual stress, uncertainty, and awkwardness that comes with finding workout buddies and groups.

As an added bonus, TigerGainz's directory permits you to see the profiles of all workout-buddy-or-group-interested Princetonians (not just those you matched with).

By finding the best workout buddies and groups, TigerGainz helps Princetonians stay active through better exercise experiences that are:

- more consistent because buddies and groups keep each other accountable

- of higher quality since studies show that working out with others can increase the duration and intensity by up to 200% (Kansas State University)

- more fun, whether you are on- or off-campus

Happy Exercising!

How Does it Work?

Upon filling out the secure sign up form (see "Get Started" button above), TigerGainz will use an algorithm that aligns preferences--including workout days, exercise type, time zone, and frequency--to find the most fitting workout partners (or groups***) from our database of participating Princetonians.

Once matches have been made, you’ll get to see the profiles, preferences, and, most importantly, contact information of your best potential workout buddies or groups***.

Then, it’s up to you to reach out to your new workout buddy or group! If you like one of your prospective matches and want to be their workout partner, please click the “Accept” button under their profile. This will remove you from the request database.

This will send an email to your new workout buddy or group telling them that you are their new workout partner and it will give them your email. On their end, they can view your profile if they log into the TigerGainz website. Note: your new workout partner OR you, if you are the person who receives the match, will ALSO be removed from the request database.

Be on the lookout for these emails from no.reply.tigergainz@gmail.com

(The top sample email is an example of an email the person in the directory would receive. The bottom sample emails is a confirmation email for the person actively seeking a match with the algorithm)

Alternatively, if you’re still interested in other options, you can check out the TigerGainz directory for all workout-buddy-or-group-interested Princetonians.

If you are no longer interested in finding Princeton workout buddies or groups, we will remove you from our database. If you wish to be removed from the database, please go to the Database section of our website and click “Take me off the database please” button.

Conversely, if you are unsatisfied with your matches or have no matches, TigerGainz will place you in our waiting list database where you’ll wait for someone to choose you as their workout buddy. If someone “Accepts” you as a match, TigerGainz will notify you via email of your new workout buddy and give you their contact information. Additionally, you can view the workout preferences and profile of your new workout buddy on the TigerGainz website in the “Matches” section. Please note that if someone chooses you, you will automatically be removed from the waiting list.

On the other hand, if you don’t want to wait for someone to choose you, you can remove yourself from the database and then resubmit the form to see if you have any matches. To do this, please go to the Database section of our website and click “Take me off the database please” button. Then go to the sign up form and complete it again to (hopefully) find new matches.
